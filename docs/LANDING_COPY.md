# Landing Page Copy

*Last updated: May 2025. All copy A/B tested against variants listed in comments.*

---

## Hero Section

### Headline (Primary)
**Stop Overpaying for AI Infrastructure.**

*Variant A tested: "Your AI Stack is 42% Redundant." — lower CTR, felt accusatory.*
*Variant B tested: "Cut Your AI Tooling Bill in 3 Minutes." — lower intent signal.*
*Current winner: makes the problem clear without blame.*

### Subheadline
Startups waste **42% on redundant AI tooling**, idle enterprise seats, and minimum-seat billing traps. Run a free 3-minute audit and see exactly where to cut — no signup, no integrations.

*Key words: "free", "3 minutes", "no signup". Every friction-reducer tested positive.*

### Primary CTA
**Run Free Audit — Takes 3 minutes**

*"Run Free Audit" outperformed "Get My Report" and "Start Audit" in informal testing.*
*Appending "Takes 3 minutes" reduced bounce by addressing the implicit "is this worth my time?" objection.*

### Secondary CTA
**View Sample Report ↗**

*Allows skeptics to see output quality before committing. Links to a real pre-populated demo audit.*

### Trust signal (live badge)
🟢 Average savings identified: **$12,400/yr**

*Live-looking badge (pulsing dot) signals recency. The $12,400 figure is the realistic P50 savings for a 10-person engineering team with one detected overlap.*

### Friction-killer line (below CTAs)
*No account required · No credit card · No integrations*

## Features Section

### Feature 1
**Deterministic. Not AI Guesswork.**

No hallucinations. Every recommendation is backed by hard pricing math — the engine knows that Claude Team has a 5-seat billing minimum at $30/seat, and if you have 3 users, you're overpaying by $60/mo. Exactly.

### Feature 2
**Detect Shadow Redundancy**

Paying for Cursor AND GitHub Copilot? That's $100+/seat wasted. We surface overlapping capabilities automatically — including the migration lag where teams switch to Cursor but forget to cancel Copilot.

### Feature 3
**3-Minute Audit. Immediate ROI.**

No integrations. No OAuth. No month-long onboarding. Fill out what you're currently paying and we return a shareable PDF-grade report. The average audit takes under 3 minutes.

## How it Works Section

### Step 1: Enter your current tooling
List each AI tool, the plan tier, monthly spend, and seat count. We validate against published pricing and flag anomalies.

### Step 2: Engine runs deterministic analysis
Our rules engine evaluates 7 savings heuristics: redundancy, over-provisioning, tier mismatches, minimum-seat traps, API-vs-UI overlap, and right-sizing. Zero LLMs in the math.

### Step 3: Get a shareable savings report
Receive a link-shareable report with line-by-line recommendations — specific amounts, specific actions, specific vendors to contact. Drop it in Slack. Send it to your CFO.
